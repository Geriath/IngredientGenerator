Name: Dragon's Breath
Latin Name: Draco exhalatio
Description: Dragon's Breath is a rare, glittering powder with a faint odor of sulfur and smoke. Its color ranges from a deep charcoal grey to a shining silver.

Location: Dragon's Breath can be found in the ash-covered caves of the highest mountain peaks in the world.

Rarity: Dragon's Breath is incredibly rare, and only a handful of alchemists know of its existence.

Uses: Dragon's Breath is said to possess powerful magical properties. It can be used to transmute base metals into precious ones, as well as to create powerful elixirs and potions. In addition, it can be used to augment the strength, speed, and intelligence of those who consume it.
